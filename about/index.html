About

Founded to close one gap.

Novapex exists to help mid-size regional companies build the kind of marketing systems usually reserved for companies ten times their size.

The Problem We Saw

Two bad options, and nothing in between.

Most mid-size companies are stuck choosing between a boutique social media shop that posts without a plan, or a big, faceless agency chasing bigger retainers than they can offer. Neither option gives a growing business the one thing it actually needs: a real system, built by someone who's actually in the work with them.

Novapex is the third option. Content systems, brand intelligence dashboards, and campaign mechanics, built in-house, tested in the real world, and designed to keep working long after the first campaign ends.

How We Work

Direct access. No handoffs.

Every engagement is led by senior strategists directly, never routed through a rotating account team or a junior delegate. What we build, we build ourselves, which means we understand it well enough to fix it fast when something needs adjusting.

Where We Stand

Global standard. Local edge.

We bring in what's proven to work in bigger markets, and only the parts that are genuinely usable here, right now, with the tools and budgets that actually exist today. No importing hype nobody local can act on yet, and no pretending post-and-pray is good enough either.

Quiet confidence. No hype. Either it's for you, or it isn't.
