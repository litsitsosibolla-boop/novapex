What's Actually Happening

Picture the next grant cycle. A funder is deciding between your organization and one doing similar work. The other organization has been quietly sharing real, specific stories all year, one life at a time, so the funder already feels emotionally connected before the proposal even lands. Your proposal has to do all the convincing alone, in a document, competing against a relationship that's already been built.

The second one is what actually unlocks the next grant, and it's usually sitting right there, already having happened, uncaptured.
