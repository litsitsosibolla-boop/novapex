The Situation

The Usual Approach

Most pages in this position do the obvious thing.

Post whenever something happens. Hope it lands. Measure success by likes. That's post-and-pray, and it rarely builds anything that lasts past the next post.

The System

Every post followed the same repeatable shape.

01

02

03

Land somewhere that built trust.Not just attention for its own sake, credibility that carried into the next post.
